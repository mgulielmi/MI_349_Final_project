Parasite (2019)

Parasite (2019) Is a Korean dark comedy/thriller film directed by Bong Joon-ho. The film follows a lower-caste Korean family who, one by one, become part of a wealthy family's staff through means of fraud and manipulation. The film was met by international acclaim for Joon-oh's camera tecniques and sotrytelling. It went on to become the first non-English movie to win the award for best picture at the Academy Awards. Parasite is widely considered to be one of the best films of not only it's year, but the 2010s as a whole.

Accolades

  • Palme d'Or; Cannes film festival (2019)
  • Academy awards; Best director, best picture, best original screenplay, best international feature film (2020)
  • AACTA awards; best Asian film (2019), best international film (2020)
